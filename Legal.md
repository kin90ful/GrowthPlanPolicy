# GrowthPlan Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how GrowthPlan ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the GrowthPlan mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account information.** When you create an account, our authentication provider collects your email address and, if you choose to provide it, your name.
- **Business details you provide.** Information you enter to generate a growth plan, such as your business name, industry, stage, revenue range, team size, goals, and challenges.
- **Subscription information.** When you purchase GrowthPlan+, our payments partner and Apple process the transaction. We receive your subscription status, not your full payment card details.
- **Usage and device data.** Basic technical information needed to operate the App reliably, such as app version and general usage events.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To generate and store your personalized growth plans.
- To create and manage your account and authenticate you.
- To process and manage your subscription and entitlements.
- To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the App.

## 3. AI Processing

To build your growth plan, the business details you provide are sent to a third-party AI provider that generates tailored recommendations. We do not use your information to train third-party AI models.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third parties to provide core functionality. Each handles data under its own privacy policy:

- **Clerk**: account authentication.
- **RevenueCat**: subscription management.
- **Apple**: in-app purchase processing.
- **OpenAI**: AI-generated growth plan content.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account and plan data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize the associated personal data, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial reasons.

## 6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the address below. You can also stop all future data collection by discontinuing use of the App and deleting your account.

## 7. Children's Privacy

GrowthPlan is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
